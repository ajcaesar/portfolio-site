Claw Machine Replica

My favorite childhood build. Replica arcade claw machine designed from scratch using servos, metal rods for the gantry, and scrapwood. Controlled via arduino & joystick. 4 servos in total, one opens claw, one raises/lowers claw and two control a rubber belt which designated the xy position of the claw. Working on modifying it with raspberry pi and cv to automatically spot and grasp objects.
