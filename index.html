Claw Machine Replica

Details

My favorite childhood build. Modeled after an arcade-style claw machine designed from scratch using servos, metal rods for the gantry, and scrapwood. Controlled via arduino & joystick. 4 servos in total, one opens claw, one raises/lowers claw and two control a rubber belt which designates the xy position of the claw. Working on modifying it with rpi and cv to automatically spot and grasp objects.
